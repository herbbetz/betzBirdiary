Apple MAC (high sierra) Terminal ssh client sends wrong locale: LC_CTYPE=UTF-8
Raspbian does not recognize this locale, so:
	in sudo nano /etc/ssh/sshd_config
	`AcceptEnv LANG` instead of `AcceptEnv LANG LC_*`
	sudo systemctl restart ssh
